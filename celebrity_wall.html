CELEBRATIONS


Our SEL programs teach that there is always something to celebrate in life. Our programs are full of joys, tears, giggles and hugs, and we celebrate all of them. For us, our beneficiaries are our celebrities whom we celebrate for who they are. We seek to bring celebrations to those who have been kept away from them for socio-economic reasons.

Cheni`s Birthday Celebration


Abandoned by her parents at an early age, Cheni is currently living with her grandfather who is old to take care of her. She is often fed by one of her aunts and sometimes accompanies them to their work. On her birthday celebration, this little celebrity was high on energy and happiness.

PS: Don't miss the effort she took to get dressed for her special day!





MCPS Plantation Drive Celebrations


The students of Meerut City Public School planted 700 trees in a neglected neighborhood as their Act of Change. The initiative was organized by the school management and its students post having two Challenge Days.
